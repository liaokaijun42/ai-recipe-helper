# AI Recipe Helper

AI Recipe Helper is a cooking assistant that helps users generate recipe ideas based on the ingredients they already have.

## Core Idea

The user enters available ingredients, chooses a cooking scenario, and receives an easy-to-follow recipe with clear steps, cooking time, difficulty, flavor profile, and plating suggestions.

## Meal Scenarios

- Light meals
- Quick meals
- Healthy meals
- Comfort food
- Budget meals
- Dating dinner
- Family meals
- High-protein meals

## Core Features

1. Ingredient-based recipe generation
2. Meal scenario selection
3. Step-by-step cooking instructions
4. Cooking time and difficulty estimate
5. Ingredient substitutions
6. Plating and presentation suggestions
7. Beginner-friendly cooking explanations

## Product Goal

Make cooking feel simple, vivid, and enjoyable, especially for people who do not know what to cook with limited ingredients.
